Display & Visibility Utilities

Resize the browser window to see elements appear and disappear at different breakpoints.
👁 d-none d-md-block — Hidden on mobile, visible from md (768px) and up.
🖥 d-none d-lg-flex — Only visible on lg (992px) and above, shown as flexbox.
📱 d-block d-md-none — Only on mobile screens (below 768px).
📟 d-none d-sm-block d-md-none — Visible only on sm breakpoint (576px–767px).
✅ d-block — Always visible at all breakpoints.
